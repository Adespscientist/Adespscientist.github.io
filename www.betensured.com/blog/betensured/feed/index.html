Here’s an overview of the latest schedules of live football matches this weekend on TV and Online. Remember to login at Betensured to view Predictions from our professional expert analysts, in order to secure your bets this weekend.

DSTV/GoTV- Supersports

Supersports have exclusive rights in Africa to broadcast all the English Premier League matches live. The weekend kicks off on Saturday 20th January with Watford vs Tottenham on SS3 at 2:30pm (CAT). The SkyBet Championship plays live on SS11. QPR vs Leeds starts at 2:30 (CAT).

The late match is Newcastle vs Chelsea at 7:30pm (CAT). For Super Sunday the blockbuster game Liverpool vs Man Utd will be live on SS3.

SS7 broadcasts live La Liga matches where fans can watch SuperCopa champions Real Madrid vs Sevilla at 5pm (CAT). They also have the rights to Serie A, shown on SS9, with Ronaldo returning in Juventus vs Parma on Sunday 21st January at 9:45pm (CAT).

StarTimes

StarTimes broadcast live German Bundesliga matches through affiliate Fox Sports who have rights in US. So viewers can watch Hertha BSC vs Bayern Munich on Sunday 21st 4:30 pm (CAT). On ST Sports Focus they show highlights of the French Ligue 1, and German Bundesliga.

On ST Sports Premium they broadcast Juventus TV, Barcelona TV, and Roma TV so fans can get exclusive content of their favorite clubs.

Streaming and Online

Soccer fans can view the live matches, stream live soccer online, and catchup on highlights using Apps such as StarTimes ON. To find matches in lower leagues, Youtube offers a great alternative. For live Spanish Segunda Division matches, LaLiga2 SmartBank Youtube channel offers free live streaming online. So you can start seeing the teams that will be playing in La Liga next season.
