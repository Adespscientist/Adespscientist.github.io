He was first in Juventus and he went to play with Real Madrid, soon he became the star of Manchester United and his leaving broke the heart of many of the fans because he was and still is the highest goal scorer. He has a number of businesses he is running, his fashion line and many others that has accumulated to giving him the net worth of $500 million.

Though he is retired and the calculation of this net worth is a combination of what he and his wife Victoria Beckham makes; she is singer/designer. His annual salary then was $50 million and as at this year, after retirement, his salary, endorsements and many other businesses in cumulative sums up to that $450 million. He is reputed to be the first English player to win big time in all the clubs he was signed in. He didn’t limit himself as he has won great trophies several times with Manchester United, Preston North End, Real Madrid, Paris Saint Germain, LA Galaxy, and Milan.

He has signed some life time deals with Pepsi, Adidas, Nike, Hawkers, Huawei and Gatorade. He is among the best players the world has ever known though he has not yet won any World Cup trophy for Argentina where he is captain but no one can deny that he has made a mark in the sands of time in the area of football, especially as one of the best strikers of all time and have scored over 400 goals in the history of La Liga.

He has won several awards. 4 UEFA Champions Leagues title, 6 copper Del Rey, 6 Golden boots and most hattricks in the Spanish La Liga.

Still on the contract he signed, he is to earn 1.7 million per year. He got other offers from Santos which he first rejected but later on took a lesser deal that made him get $4 million instead of the $20 million deal initially proposed; he signed a huge deal with Barcelona that made him earn 15 million per year in salary. He also signed some other deals and endorsements. In this year alone, he has earned about $96 million and on top of that, from his endorsements, he has earned an average of $20-40 million. All these add up to make up his net worth.

He had an early retirement due to some bad injuries but he also made money in his days as he set the pace for strikers of this time. He has a net worth of $160 million. Because of how much he scored goals, he became sought for and every club wanted to sign him. Barcelona in the 90s signed him with what was considered a huge sum at that time; $19.5 million, he spent the 96’-97’ season with them, scored the highest goals and soon moved to Milan where he also broke a record for his high fee of 27 million. He has signed several contracts, deals and endorsements with brands like Nike, Snickers and others. He also became the highest share holder by buying about 51% of the shares of the club Real Valladolid in 2018 and he assumed his duties of managing the club with a goal to have the best team produced from there.
  6. Gareth Bale is a Welsh footballer whose net worth is $125 million. One big event that qualifies him to be among the first 10 is that he signed the biggest deals ever with Real Madrid from 2013 that will be stretched through to 2020. This contract earns him a large sum of $33 million per year. He also makes about $9 million from several endorsements. His average salary is from $25-35 million.

He has a net worth of $125 million and his salary on the base level is about £15 million which is about $33 million. He currently plays with Manchester United after leaving Juventus. He signed a 5 year contract with them that started in 2016 and the agreement was to pay $28 million every year when spread out. His salary and endorsements adds up to place him on the top of the richest.

He played with them for 22 years but he later left and signed a shorter deal that got him a huge sum. He started a winery in 2010 and that adds up to his net worth.

Quite a number of clubs buying out his coaching contracts earned him about $60 million.

Zinedine Zidane is a 49 years old retired French footballer and coach who has done well for himself over the years with a net worth of $120 million through his football career and also his brand endorsement deals with popular companies like Christian Dior, Adidas, Lego and many others. His endorsement seems to have earned him way higher than football did. He got a reputation for being the most successful coach. Despite some financial scandals that ensued when he left the world of football, he remains a sports legend.

He plays for the French team Paris Saint-Germain signing a deal of $20 million annually for the space of 3 years. The future is bright in the game of football and there are high hopes that he would be part of the brightest in due time.
